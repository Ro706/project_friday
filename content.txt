Here's an example email for rescheduling a meeting or appointment:

Subject: Rescheduling [Meeting/Appointment] Request

Dear [Name],

I hope this email finds you well. Unfortunately, [Original Date/Time] has fallen through, and I need to reschedule our [Meeting/Appointment]. I was wondering if it would be possible to schedule an alternative time that suits you.

Would you be available on [Proposed Date/Time]? If not, please let me know a few alternative dates and times that work for you, and I'll do my best to accommodate them.

Thank you for your understanding and flexibility.

Best regards,
[Your Name]

Alternatively, you could also use a more concise version:

Subject: Rescheduling [Meeting/Appointment]

Hi [Name],

I need to reschedule our [Meeting/Appointment] from [Original Date/Time] to a new time. Would you be available on [Proposed Date/Time] or would you prefer to suggest an alternative?

Thanks,
[Your Name]

Remember to customize the email to fit your specific needs and tone.
